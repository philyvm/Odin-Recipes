Yaki Udon

By Joshua Weissman

Homemade stir fry noodle recipes like yaki udon will be a revolutionary moment for many people who are just about to start cooking, or already cooks. It's a weeknight and a weekend meal

Ingredients

Yaki Udon Noodle Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce -29g
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce - 28g
  • 1 tablespoon mirin - 14g
  • 2 tablespoons shirodashi -24g
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar -12g
  • 1 teaspoon palm sugar - 35g
